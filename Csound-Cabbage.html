Making Plugins using Cabbage and Csound

During my 7th Semester at Berklee, I studied under the direction of Dr. Richard Boulanger, where I learned about the Csound Language and later the Cabbage frontend.

Admittably, it was one of the most intense and challenging classes I had ever taken. However, I ended up with indisputable and invaluable skills, as well as a small handful of plugins that I had created.

If you would like to try out my Cabbage plugins, you may, and you may have noticed that I included a download for them here on this page. There are two points of contention however. The plugins here have been exported for OSX only in VST format. You also must first install Csound onto your computer if you want them to work within your DAW.

Many Thanks must also be paid to the Cabbage Forum, where I had spent many hours troubleshooting my problems.
